# Colors
Kitchen comes with a few different colors to choose from.

## Available Colors

Colors vary depending on the theme you are using. You can find the default implemented themes in the [theme documentation](/docs/guides/theme-builder).

### Accents

Kitchen also comes with a few accent colors to choose from.
Accents aren't colors that vary, they are used to highlight certain elements.

  code={`

</Container>
`}
